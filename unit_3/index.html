If it swims, we will fish for it!

High Adventure Sportfishing is full time professional charter and guide service based in Pulaski, NY. Lake Ontario's Eastern Basin has developed into one of the finest fisheries in the country. Putting this, together with over 30 years of experience pursuing Trout and Salmon, ensures you a day of fishing you'll never forget.

Salmon River and Tributaries

September - November

The season begins in early September when King Salmon start to migrate up the Salmon River in Pulaski, NY. High Adventure Sportfishing targets trophy Kings with fly rods and bright colored flies. Once we have sufficient water flow, we put the drift boat into service and use high action plugs to take these aggressive, territorial fish. Salmon will also strike plugs.Spend a day with High Adventure Sportfishing and we will show you how to catch Salmon from bank or boat.

By mid-October, large numbers of egg-gobbling Steelhead and Brown Trout start to enter the rivers and migrate through the winter months. A float trip aboard our heated drift boat is a great way to learn the river and expierence its beauty.

By mid-March, the Steelhead are spawning. As these fish drop back to the lake, they feed ferociously to regain strength and body weight.

Between April and May, action can be spectacular. These fish will take all the offerings: plugs, spinners, eggs, and live bait.

Oneida Lake Fish

May - June

This is a no pressure, relaxing, fishing trip. The abundance of Walleye in Oneida Lake is amazing. A maximum of four guests can fit in the 22' Pathfinder Bay Boat. There is a three fish limit of eyes with many more released throughout the day. Throw in some jumbo Perch, White Bass, and a few Smallies, and you have the makings of an action-packed trip on the water. Spinning rods, casting rods, bait, and lures are provided.

Florida Backwaters

December - February

I now spend three months a year plying some of the most fertile water in southwest Florida. I am docked in Pine Island, which is just west of Fort Myers. On this trip, we fish for Snook, Reds, Sheepshead, Mackerel, snapper, sharks, trout and anything else that swims! The Florida sunshine and calm waters make for easy fishing and almost everything you catch you can eat. Walk the beautiful beaches and keep an eye out for dolphins, manatees, sting-rays, and birds. Let us plan that special winter get-a-way for you, your family, and your friends.

Captain Troy Creasy

Troy Creasy is a full-time dedicated professional, pursuing trout and salmon for 28 years, with 25 years guiding experience. He is on the water year round and will provide you with a complete charter service. HIGH ADVENTURE SPORTFISHING is proud to use only the best equipment available.

Captain Troy is a member of the Mustad, Maxima, Luhr Jensen, Pro-Troll & Pautzke Baits Pro Staff. Captain Troy has been very active in the Fish community since 1987.

He has served as president of the Oswego County River Guides Association. He is a member of the NYS Fisheries Congress, Lake Ontario Sportfishing Promotion Council, Eastern Lake Ontario Salmon and Trout Association and the New York State Writers Association. He is also an editor for Lake Ontario Outdoors magazine, a quarterly publication promoting outdoor sports in the Lake Ontario region.

Whether you are looking to troll for trout & salmon, walk the streams, drift the rivers, fly fish, float fish, jig for walleye or spend some time in Florida fishing the backwaters of Pine Island Sound. Captain Troy is your go to guide for all of your fishing adventures.
